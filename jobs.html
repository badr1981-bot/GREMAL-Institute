Call for Applications – Join GREMAL Institute Founding Team

GREMAL Institute– the Global Research for Epistemics and Modalities of Algorithmic Life – is launching its foundational phase and invites committed individuals to apply for key leadership and coordination roles within the think tank.

As we establish our core team, we seek individuals with strong vision and collaborative spirit.

Available Positions

  • Research Director: Oversees research programs, publications, and calls for papers.
  • Strategic Partnerships Director:Manages relations with foundations, universities, think tanks, and donors.
  • Scientific Outreach & Partnerships Lead: Forges institutional alliances and coordinates collaborative initiatives.
  • Community Engagement Manager: Designs public engagement strategies and builds dialogue with wider audiences.
  • Program Director:Coordinates cross-cutting initiatives (conferences, workshops, publications).

What We Offer

  • - A collaborative and intellectually vibrant environment.
  • - Recognition as a founding contributor to GREMAL Institute.
  • - Remote-friendly, flexible commitment structures.
  • - Opportunities to influence international policy and academic discourse.

Application Process

Interested applicants should send:

  • A brief statement of interest (max 1 page)
  • A CV or résumé
  • (Optional) A writing sample or portfolio
Deadline: September 12, 2025
Start of engagement: From September 2025 (flexible)
Submit via email to: jobs@gremal-intitute.org
Subject: Application – [Position Title]
