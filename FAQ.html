Frequently Asked Questions

Need answers? We've compiled the most common questions about GREMAL Institute.

What is GREMAL Institute?

GREMAL is a transdisciplinary think tank focused on global governance, law, cosmorealism, virtuactuality and digital transformation.

How can I become a member?

You can apply via our Join Us page. Applications are reviewed quarterly.

Are your publications open access?

Yes, most of our research outputs are available freely under Creative Commons licenses.

Do you organize events or workshops?

Absolutely! Check our Events section regularly for upcoming conferences and calls for papers.
